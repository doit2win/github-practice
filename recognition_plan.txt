
# One-Page Recognition Plan: Lucas Oil Civic Education & Small Business Academy

- **Program Naming:** The initiative will be publicly branded as the **Lucas Oil Civic Education & Small Business Academy** on all curricula, websites, event banners, press releases and reports.
- **Logo & Branding:** Lucas Oil’s logo will be prominently featured on printed materials, student kits, digital platforms and co-branded merchandise such as tote bags and notebooks distributed at events.
- **Media & Publicity:** All press releases and social media posts will acknowledge Lucas Oil’s lead sponsorship. Students will record thank-you videos and testimonials for sharing on Lucas Oil channels.
- **Events & Presentations:** Lucas Oil representatives will be invited to speak at the annual student showcase and other key program events. A plaque recognizing Lucas Oil’s contribution will be displayed at the We The Kids headquarters and at major events.
- **Reporting & Stewardship:** We The Kids will provide quarterly progress reports and a comprehensive final impact report containing photos, data and student stories. Lucas Oil will be offered opportunities to co-author blog posts or podcast segments to highlight the partnership.
